Apps for iOS (iPhone and iPod Touch)

Mart's Lists are smart lists

Mart's Lists lets you create and manage lists easily. It provides a convenient pad-like way to browse through your lists. If you allow it to, Mart's Lists will learn automatically if an item is required regularly and in which interval. And Mart's Lists supports dictation of your lists to make your life even more comfortable!

"Easter Eggs"

In nearly all of my programs so called Easter Eggs are hidden. Easter Eggs are undocumented features in e.g. software, which display certain text or reveal photos. Usually Easter Eggs are quite good hidden and because of this I made a page on how to find Easter Eggs in my programs.
