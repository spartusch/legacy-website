Welcome

Nice to see you here and welcome to the homepage of Stefan Partusch. See the table of contents for detailed information about what to find here. This website is primarily for the programs I've written and which are offered for free. Many of these programs are open source, i.e. their source code is freely available to the public. Everything on this website is at least freeware and therefore completely free to use. But be aware that I can't provide any warranty for the programs. Please feel free to email me your thoughts and suggestions. Simply use the email address from the about page to do so.
