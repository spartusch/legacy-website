How to find Easter Eggs

In nearly all of my programs so called Easter Eggs are hidden. Easter Eggs are undocumented features in e.g. software, which display certain text or reveal photos, when found and activated. But usually Easter Eggs are quite good hidden and therefore somtimes hard to find.
Thus I decided to provide you with some disguised tips where and how to find Easter Eggs in my programs. Below is a list of all programs with hidden Easter Eggs along with a tip. Have fun searching the Eggs!

DevCont

A name commands the address.

Japtra

Beauty in the wrong language is a promise of love.

PTInfo Munich

The nonsensical return is a joke.
