Information about ...

... this website

The aim of this website is to provide a central platform to publish the programs I've written so far. Nevertheless the currently available programs aren't all programs I've made and published. More recent and future programs are Free Software and Open Source. I hope you enjoy my programs and that you find them useful. See my email address below if you want to contact me!

The address "home.arcor.de/partusch" is the home of this website since December 12, 2001. Though the site's contents have changed or were expanded from time to time, its former design remained until October 2004, when it was completely replaced by another design using XHTML and CSS2. The actual contents are stored in a handy and custom XML format, which is transformed into XHTML using XSLT and Perl.

Contact

If you have questions about this website, its programs or if you just want to drop a line contact me via partusch@arcor.de. Your comments are always appreciated! Or follow me on Twitter: @spartusch.
