DevCont - Easter Egg

That's me in elementary school wearing my favourite Mickey Mouse T-Shirt :-)

In case you're interested: DevCont was formerly known as TCP/UDP Connector and is written in C++ using Win32API (but not OOP). In Version 1.0.0 the code had a length of about 1345 lines.
